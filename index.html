INTERNSHIP PROGRAMME

OVERVIEW

Gopali Youth Welfare Society believes that students have a strong potential to bring a change in the society. The enthusiasm within them is unmatched and can have effects beyond boundaries! As a result of this, Gopali Youth Welfare Society is taking an opportunity to make use of this energy and provide help to the weaker sections of the society through its Internship Program 2020. It is a chance to collaborate with one of the largest student run NGOs of India and have an amazing experience with the team. As a part of the Internship Program, you get a chance to work with us on our projects, contribute to it and participate in experiencing the bliss of the social initiative.

SKILLS AND INCENTIVES
Learning Session

  • An opportunity to work with specialized teams of India’s one of the largest student run NGOs.
  • A chance to participate in a real life project which will be used to make an impact at the ground level.
  • An opportunity to attend the learning sessions by the esteemed professors, and experienced team of Gopali Youth Welfare Society.
Mentorship

  • An opportunity to work under the guidance of an experienced individual who would provide constructive feedback on your work, be a consultant and a role model at the same time.
  • The mentor would help you to develop new skills, gain experience and provide you with a realistic preview of the workplace.
  • The mentor would help you identify your current strengths and weaknesses and provide you with challenges that will foster professional development.
Recognition, Certification and Awards

  • Get recognised by Gopali Youth Welfare Society at its social media handles, which have a reach to around 20k individuals including esteemed alumni and professor base of IIT Kharagpur.
  • Certification from India’s one of the Largest Student Run NGO, which will enhance your profile for higher studies opportunities and job profiles.
  • Best Interns in all the domains would be recognised and provided with a token of appreciation.

What will be the overall procedure for recruitment?
After we receive all the applications, we will have final selections amongst them based on interviews to shortlist the final interns who would be working with us.

  • An opportunity to work with specialized teams of India’s one of the largest student run NGOs.
  • A chance to participate in a real life project which will be used to make an impact at the ground level.
  • An opportunity to attend the learning sessions by the esteemed professors, and experienced team of Gopali Youth Welfare Society.
Networking:
  • Networking opportunity with various stakeholders of Gopali Youth Welfare Society.
